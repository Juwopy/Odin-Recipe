Description:

Chicken Alfredo is a quick and easy to make comfort dish that can feed a whole family. With thick soft noodles, rich and creamy sauce, and delicious chicken, you will be satisified quickly.

Ingredients:

  • 16 ounces dry fettuccine
  • 1 pound chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 clove minced garlic
  • 2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 cups parmesan cheese
    1 tsp
  • italian seasoning
  • salt
  • pepper
  • garlic powder
  • olive oil

Steps:

  1. Season the chicken, then pan-fry in oil and butter, for 6 minutes a side, until nicely browned, and rest for a few minutes until cutting into pieces
  2. Cook the Fettuccine al dente, according to package instructions.
  3. Using the same pan you used to cook the chicken, the cream, butter, and seasonings are incorporated and simmered on medium-low heat until thickened, then the Parmesan is added in until melted and smooth.
  4. add in the chicken if you want it in the sauce, and then add in the pasta and toss until combined
  5. Garnish with parsley, more Parmesan, and black pepper if desired.
