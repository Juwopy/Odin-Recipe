Description:

Hearty Noodles is a twist on traditional packet ramen. adding extra protein and with an ailment-soothing broth this dish is perfect for sick-days or late-night studying.

Ingredients:

  • Koka or top ramen noodles(chicken preferred
  • 1 cup chopped lettuce
  • 1-2 eggs(medium
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • roughly chopped fresh parsley
    1 tsp of
  • black pepper
  • turmeric
  • paprika
  • garlic granules
  • tomato paste

Steps:

  1. In a pot combine all seasonings + ramen seasoning pack and boil until bubbling
  2. add ramen, cooke for 2 minutes
  3. add eggs and peas, cook further 2 minutes
  4. add lettuce
  5. enjoy!(remember to slurp! It's good manners!
