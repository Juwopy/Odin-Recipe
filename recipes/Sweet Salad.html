ODIN RECIPES!!!!

Description:

Sweet potato salad is a compound of interesting flavors that is beginner friendly. This dish is recommmended for a refreshing breakfast or a midday pick-me-up

Ingredients:

  • 2 sweet potatoes(medium - large)
  • 1 onion(red preferred)
  • 1 eggplant(medium - large
  • 1/2 cup quinoa or couscous
  • 3 handfuls of baby carrots
  • roughly chopped fresh basil
  • roughly chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup syrup or honey
  • 2 handfuls of walnuts
    1 tsp of
  • tumeric
  • black pepper
  • salt
  • paprika
  • garlic
  • olive oil

Steps:

  1. Peel and dice sweet potat, onion and eggplant
  2. salt eggplant until bitterness has been drawn out
  3. combine in oven tray with olive oil + spices
  4. put in oven for 200-300F for 20-30 minutes
  5. cook quinoa on med-high heat for 15-20 minutes
  6. roast walnuts on pan, roughly chop, fluff quinoa with fork
  7. combine everything & enjoy!
