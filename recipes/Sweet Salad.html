ODIN RECIPES!!!!

Description:

Sweet potato salad is a compound of interesting flavors that is beginner friendly. This dish is recommmended for a refreshing breakfast or a midday pick-me-up

Ingredients:

  • 2 sweet potatoes(medium - large)
  • 1 onion(red preferred)
  • 1 eggplant(medium - large
  • 1/2 cup quinoa or couscous
  • 3 handfuls of baby carrots
  • roughly chopped fresh basil
  • roughly chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup syrup or honey
  • 2 handfuls of walnuts
    1 tsp of
  • tumeric
  • black pepper
  • salt
  • paprika
  • garlic
  • olive oil
